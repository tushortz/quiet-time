2SA 18

2SA 18:25 Basically if a man is running alone when bringing news of war then he has good news to tell and vice-versa Related verses: 2 Samuel 18:25. See notes 2SA 18:22 In war, there was usually a runner who comes back home to bring the news of the outcome. Depending on the way the runner ran, people could detect of it was good or bad news. This was the similar techniques used to explain the gospel in Isaiah 52:7.
