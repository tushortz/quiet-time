MAT 26

MAT 26:55 Day after day Jesus taught in the temple Related verses: Matthew 26:55. See notes MAT 26:44 Jesus had said where two or three are gathered in his name, the father would answer the prayers. On this principle, Jesus took Peter, James and John so they could intercede for him not to face the suffering on the cross. Jesus returned to his disciples to receive confirmation of his answered prayer but the answer was no, they were all sleeping so prayer couldn’t be answered.
