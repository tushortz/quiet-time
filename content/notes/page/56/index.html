1KI 20

1KI 20:39 The prophet represented Ahab, the man to be guarded was Ben-Hadad and the man who brought the man to be guarded was God. God had delivered Ben-Hadad into the hands of Ahab to be devoted for destruction but Ahab made a pact with him and let him escape and Ahab unknowingly pronounced judgement on himself just as David had done when God also sent a prophet to him.
