MAT 1

MAT 1:21 Joseph needed to name Jesus for Jesus to be legal on earth. everything that God created was given a name and remained under his dominion. everything that was kept under the dominion of man was given a name by man. Name reveals attributes and personality and Jesus being named by Joseph gave him the personality as saviour. The angels couldn’t name Jesus so a man had to do it.

PSA 121

PSA 121:2 The hills of Jerusalem (City of peace) is where the high places of worship was located before Solomon built the temple on mount Moriah. Looking up to the hills is looking up to God and trusting him for provision. I cannot rely on man to provide for me but only God is the source and provider of my needs and is very happy to do so. God will be there to keep the path I walk when I live righteously.
