LUK 20

Because it is public, it is visible to the public and humbling because people will see you and will know you agree that you are a sinner. it took humility to be baptised. Christ was humble enough to get baptised. John was baptised from heaven (ordained by God. he was filled with the Holy Spirit from birth).
