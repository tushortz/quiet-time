## JOS 18:3

Problem
- 7 tribes haven't possessed their land

Cause 
- Procrastination, 
- laziness and 
- mediocrity 

Solution
- Strategic thinking (come up with a plan)
- Allocate reliable/faithful men for task at hand
- Survey the land
- Depend on God for action to take
