PSA 42

PSA 42:7 Be strong in the Lord. Not in the gym, or physics, fighting, sports or in PhD. Don’t get me wrong. Being strong in education or gym is good but the main aim is to be strong in the Lord and in the power of his might. You must know God before you can be strong in the Lord. Example, if you don’t know about the country’s system e.g. Free education till certain level in some countries, you would be at loss.
